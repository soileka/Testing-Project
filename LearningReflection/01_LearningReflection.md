## 2. How did you write or manage your test case? Describe the process.

I started the test case planning by doing the simplest positive test case first. This helped me familiarize myself with the webapp and I was able to come upp with ideas on what to test. I made notes of all the fields and options that I wanted to test and started writing the titles and descriptions for them. I then wrote and tested each one of them step-by-step and added necessary variables according to need. I also made improvements to my descriptions and test plans according to need.
